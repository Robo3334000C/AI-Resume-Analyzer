# Design System

This document outlines the core design principles and specifications for our product, ensuring a consistent and harmonious user experience across all touchpoints.

## Theme Overview

Our design system uses a **light color mode** for a clean and accessible interface. The overall aesthetic is **balanced and modern**, with a focus on readability and usability.

## Colors

The color palette is derived to provide a vibrant yet professional feel.

-   **Primary Color:** `#B7DE05` - This bright, energetic green is used for key interactive elements like buttons, calls-to-action, and primary navigation. It signifies importance and action.
-   **Secondary Color:** `#6C7F28` - A more subdued, earthy green that complements the primary color. It's suitable for less prominent UI elements, chips, and secondary actions, providing visual hierarchy.
-   **Tertiary Color:** `#C3D8FF` - A soft, cool blue that serves as an accent color for highlights, badges, or decorative elements, adding a touch of contrast and freshness.
-   **Neutral Color:** `#767868` - A warm gray that forms the base for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements, ensuring readability and a calming backdrop.

## Typography

We prioritize clarity and modern appeal through our font choices. All text elements utilize the **Inter** typeface, known for its legibility and versatility across various sizes and contexts.

-   **Headline Font:** Inter - Used for all headings and titles, ensuring strong visual impact and clear hierarchy.
-   **Body Font:** Inter - Applied to all paragraph text and longer content blocks, optimized for comfortable reading.
-   **Label Font:** Inter - Employed for form labels, small text, and UI component labels, maintaining consistency and clarity.

## Shape and Form

Our UI elements feature **moderate roundedness (2)**, striking a balance between modern sharpness and a friendly, approachable aesthetic. This applies to buttons, cards, input fields, and other interactive components.

## Spacing

The design system incorporates **normal spacing (2)**, providing adequate whitespace within and between UI elements. This ensures a clean layout, reduces visual clutter, and enhances readability without feeling too dense or overly spacious.
